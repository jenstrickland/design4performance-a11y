Design for Performance & Accessibility

When do you think about performance and accessibility?

If you're like many teams, you likely optimize performance and add accessibility accommodations in the development phase, after stakeholder sign-off. At this point, the dev team is on the hook for design decisions which may not optimally balance performance and aesthetics, nor consider the experience for users of assistive technology.

This talk describes a design process prioritizing performance and accessibility from the beginning of your project.

  • "design in the browser" methodology
  • QA for accessibility and performance throughout
