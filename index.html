Prioritize Performance & Accessibility from the Start

When do you think about performance and accessibility?

If you're like many teams, you likely optimize performance and add accommodations for accessibility in the development phase, after stakeholder sign-off. At this point, the dev team is on the hook for design decisions which may not optimally balance performance and aesthetics, nor consider the experience for users of assistive technology.

This talk describes a design process prioritizing performance and accessibility from the beginning.

  • "design in the browser" methodology
  • QA for accessibility and performance throughout

Marcy Sutton wrote about how sites with performance issues are often nightmares for accessibility in her post, Accessibility and Performance.
